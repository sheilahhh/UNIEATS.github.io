    What is UNI EATS? Learn all about us below

    UNI EATS is a web application developed specifically for USIU students and faculty to enable them remotely access services from the cafeterias within the campus.

    The aim of this application is to:

    • Reduce the time taken queuing at the cafeterias.
    • Improve customer services.

    Chicken Pilau

    Swahili dish

    This is a swahili dish. It is rich in spices and flavour. It consists of chicken marintaed in a variety of flavours and mixed with rice

    Cassava leaves and rice with plantains

    Cassava leaves(sombe) is a traditional Congolose delicacy.

    It is grounded with a a variety of spices and is served with rice,fufu and many more. Enjoy it with rice and plantains

    Tilapia, ugali and greens

    Enjoy this luo traditional delicacy

    It is rich in falvour and nutrients and is cooked as it is along the Lake Victoria Shores!

    Mukimo,beef, and chapati

    Mukimo is a kenyan traditional meal made from mashing potatoes with green vegeatables. Enjoy it with a serving of beef and chapati.

    Chineese rice and egg

    This is a traditional chineese meal.

    It is prepared by stir frying rice with eggs,soy sauce and vegetables like carrots, peas, pepper. It is garnished with sliced green onions if preferred
